Introduction & Overview

About us

At Seoul & Bean, we believe in delivering an authentic Korean culinary dessert experience. Our story started in the heart of Seoul and now we bring the traditional flavors blended with a modern twist right to your plate. Each dessert is prepared with love, keeping in mind the rich cultural heritage of Korea. We invite you to take a journey with us through the bustling streets of Seoul, one bite at a time.

Vision and Mission

Vision: To be recognized as the foremost authority on Korean cuisine worldwide, delivering authentic flavors with unparalleled quality.

Mission: Our mission is to offer our customers a dining experience that exceeds their expectations by offering the finest quality food, service, and atmosphere. We aim to celebrate and spread the Korean culinary culture globally.

Origin

Bingsu, a popular Korean shaved ice dessert adorned with an array of toppings, dates back to the Joseon dynasty. Initially, it was a simple dish of shaved ice with sweetened red beans. Over time, Bingsu has evolved, and now you can find a wide variety of flavors and toppings. Seoul & Bean's Bingsu is a homage to this rich history, and we take pride in perfecting every bowl to transport you straight to Korea.
