Our Approach & Strategy

Localizing Bingsu

In today's era of globalization, it's vital for any business to resonate with the local community. Understanding this, we aim to ensure that our Bingsu caters to the American palate. Hiring native-born American staff allows us to bridge cultural gaps and bring a localized touch to our traditional Korean delicacy. These employees, well-versed in both cultures, will play a pivotal role in offering guidance and educating our customers about the rich heritage of our products.

Menu Presentation

We understand that a delightful dining experience starts way before the first bite. With that in mind, our Bingsu cafe differentiates itself by offering both communal and personal serving dishes, ensuring everyone feels at home. For many, Bingsu may be a new experience; our menu simplifies this introduction. With vivid imagery and concise explanations accompanying each item, customers can order with confidence, knowing exactly what awaits them.

Seasonal Specials

Embracing local festivities is essential in forging a bond with our customers. While Bingsu is inherently Korean, our special seasonal variants will echo the spirit of American celebrations. Whether it's a Bingsu with pumpkin flavors for Halloween or a red-white-and-blue rendition for the Fourth of July, we aim to blend traditions beautifully.

Promotions

In a digital age, promotions play an integral role in business growth. Our digital presence, particularly on platforms like Instagram and Facebook, will be leveraged to offer value-added services to our customers. From exclusive 10% discount coupons to loyalty programs, our aim is to not just attract, but also retain our valued patrons.

Bringing the best of Seoul's flavors with a modern twist.
